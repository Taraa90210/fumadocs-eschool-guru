To use the **eSchool Mobile** application, make sure you have an official account registered by the school or eSchool admin.

## 1. Download Application

Download the **eSchool Mobile** application via:

* [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=id.ac.eschool.GuruStaff.android&hl=id) for Android devices
* [Apple App Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=id.ac.eschool.GuruStaff.android&hl=id) for iOS devices

Search with the keyword: **"eSchool Mobile"**

## 2. Login with Official Account

After the application is installed:

1. Open the eSchool Mobile application
2. Press the **"Login"** button on the home page
3. Enter the **username and password** you received from the school
4. If this is your first login, you may be asked to:
   * Change password
   * Verify identity or basic account data

> Login accounts can only be used if they have been **activated by the school admin or eSchool operator**.

## 3. Choose Your School

If your account is connected to **more than one school** (e.g. as a parent with children in several schools, or a teacher with more than one assignment), then after logging in you will see the **"Select School"** page.

* Select the name of the school you want to access right now
* You can **switch schools** at any time via the profile or settings menu
* Make sure you select the appropriate school to view the correct data, classes, and activities

***

## Login Problems?

If you experience problems logging in, here are the steps you can take:

* **Forgot Password?**\
  Use the **"Forgot Password"** feature on the application login page.\
  Follow the reset instructions that will be sent via the registered **email**.
* **Cannot Access Account?**\
  Contact directly:
  * **School Admin** (to check your account status)
  * Or contact **eSchool Support Team** via the [**Help**](https://www.eschool.ac.id/#contact-us) menu
