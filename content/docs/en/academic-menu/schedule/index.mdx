The **"Teacher Schedule"** menu in the eSchool Mobile application is a special feature for teachers to view and manage daily teaching schedules in real-time, directly from their grasp.

# Key Features:

1. **Daily Navigation**:\
    Select active days (Monday–Friday) to display teaching schedules on that day.
2. **Clear Time Range**:\
    Schedules are displayed vertically by hour, making them easy to read and follow.
3. **Complete Information per Session**:\
    * **Lesson Hour** (e.g. 07.15 WIB)
    * **Subject** (e.g. PJOK – Theory)
    * **Class Name** (e.g. X ELIN B or X METRO B)

# Benefits for Teachers:

1. **Quick access to today's and other days' schedules**
2. **No need to print manually or check the school schedule board**
3. **Always ready when moving classes, even in the middle of activities**
4. **Helps time management and teaching material preparation**

\
**Usage Suggestions:**

* Use this feature every morning to see the order of classes today.
* Suitable to be paired with the "Assignments & Assessment" feature to give assignments directly to the class after the session is over.
* Make sure your application is always connected so that the schedule can be updated automatically by the school admin.
