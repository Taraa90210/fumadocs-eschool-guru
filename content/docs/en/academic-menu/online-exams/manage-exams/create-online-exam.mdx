## Build Your Digital Exam in a Few Easy Steps

The **Create Online Exam** feature gives freedom in determining exam parameters in detail with an efficient and clear form display.

# Steps to Create Online Exam:

1. **Basic Information**

        Determine:

        * **Class and subject**
        * **Exam title**
        * **Exam key (for participant access)**

        Exam keys can be generated automatically or adjusted manually to maintain confidentiality.

2. **Exam Details**

        Set:

        * **Start date and time**
        * **End date and time**
        * **Maximum exam duration (up to 999 minutes)**

        This gives full control over working time and exam validity.

3. **Finalization**

        After all parameters are set, simply press **"Create Exam"** to publish the exam instantly to students in the relevant class. The exam will automatically enter the list and can be edited at any time.
