## Optimize Extracurricular Management and Student Participation Records

The **Extracurricular** feature in the eSchool Mobile application is designed to help teachers and extracurricular supervisors manage all non-academic activities more structuredly. From monitoring the member list, managing schedules, to recording attendance, all can be done easily through one integrated platform.

# Main Features:

1. **Extracurricular Member Management**\
    Easily monitor the list of students joined in various extracurricular activities. Supervisors can view student profiles and their status in each activity.
2. **Submission & Approval Process**\
    Includes a feature to manage student join requests. Supervisors can approve or reject submissions based on quota and activity requirements.
3. **Flexible Activity Scheduling**\
    Arrange and publish activity schedules so that students and supervisors can coordinate better. Includes information on time, location, and type of activity.
4. **Digital Presence (Attendance)**\
    Record activity attendance digitally. The system will automatically recap attendance data, making it easier to evaluate student activeness at the end of the period.
5. **Real-Time Data Integration**\
    All activity data and attendance are automatically connected to the school's central system, facilitating reporting to management and parents.

# Benefits:

1. Increase efficiency in extracurricular administration without manual notes.
2. Provide transparency in the member selection and approval process.
3. Facilitate supervisors in monitoring student participation and attendance.
4. Support the development of student interests and talents through better-managed documentation.
