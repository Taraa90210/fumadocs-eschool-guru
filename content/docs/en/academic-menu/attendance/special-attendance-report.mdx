**Monitor Student Attendance Statistics Accurately and Interactively**

The **Attendance Report** menu in eSchool Mobile is designed to facilitate teachers and school staff in **viewing student attendance recaps in real-time**, complete with data filters, statistical percentages, and details per student.

# Key Features:

1. **Filter by Class & Date**\
    Select specific classes and dates to display relevant and focused attendance data. Ideal for daily, weekly, or incidental reports.
2. **Visual Attendance Summary**\
    Displays attendance summary charts directly with percentages and total students for each category:
    * **Present**
    * **Sick**
    * **Permission**
    * **Absent (Alpha)**
3. **Student Attendance List**\
    Displayed in a neat table with each student's attendance status, complete with different icons and colors to speed up identification.
4. **Special Status Filter**\
    Users can choose to only display students who are present, sick, permitted, or absent separately.
5. **“All” Display Mode**\
    To review the complete list of students along with their attendance status on the selected day.

# Feature Benefits:

1. Facilitate teachers in conducting **daily attendance analysis**
2. Accelerate reporting to homeroom teachers, Administration, or principals
3. Provide data that is **ready to be used for monthly recaps or class evaluations**
4. Minimize recording errors through automated systems & visual validation

**Usage Tips:**

* Use class and date filters to focus on specific activity reports.
* Suitable for use before compiling counseling reports, report cards, or disciplinary coaching.
* Report data is integrated directly from attendance recording results performed by teachers in the previous feature.
