## Organize and Monitor All Face-to-Face Exam Activities Efficiently

The **Offline Exam Schedule** menu is designed to provide comprehensive visibility to teachers and students regarding the implementation of offline exams. With a clean and informative interface, users can monitor daily schedules, details of subjects tested, and exam status in real-time.

# Key Features

1. **Real-Time Exam Status**

    Every ongoing exam will be marked with an **"In Progress"** label, making it easier for users to identify active activities on that day.

2. **Date and Day Details**

    The system displays schedule information in detail:

    * Day and date of implementation
    * Exam start and end dates
    * Total exam duration

    All arranged chronologically for easy browsing, especially during busy exam periods.

3. **Integrated Classes and Subjects**

    * Show **exam participant classes** (example: XI TKJ - ID)
    * Number and types of **subjects** tested
    * Additional information such as start time, end time, and **minimum passing grade**

4. **Grades & Evaluation**

    Each exam is also accompanied by:

    * **Maximum Total Score** — to assess student exam results
    * **Passing Grade** — as a competency standard that must be achieved

    These grades are displayed directly in the exam card to facilitate result monitoring.

5. **Detailed Navigation**

    The **“More”** button allows users to see:

    * Details of subjects tested
    * Implementation time per subject
    * Specific durations and evaluation metrics used

6. **Structured Daily Schedule**

    In one view, users can see:

    * Exams that are ongoing and will take place
    * Schedule division per day and per subject
    * Colors and icons that distinguish exam status and important information
