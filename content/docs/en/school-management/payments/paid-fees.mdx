# Main Features:

1. **Year & Status Filters**\
    Browse payment data based on the academic year and payment status (paid or unpaid).
2. **Complete Payment List**\
    Display all students with payment information such as:
    * Total Fee
    * Amount Paid
    * Remaining Balance
    * Status (Paid / Unpaid)
3. **Clear Payment Details**\
    Each student has detailed payment information including student ID, class, and other specifics.
4. **Download Payment Receipt**\
    A dedicated button is available to download the payment receipt as digital proof of transaction.

**Usage Example:**

> "With this feature, school admins can instantly identify which students have not yet paid their monthly fees for a specific month, without having to manually check each one by one."
