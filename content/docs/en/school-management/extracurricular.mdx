The Extracurricular feature in this application provides ease of management for non-academic activities. School admins can manage activity administration ranging from schedules and student registration to attendance recapitulation.

# Main Features

1. **Manage Extracurriculars**

        This menu is the data hub for all school extracurricular activities.

        **Adding New Extracurriculars**

        *   Tap the **+ (Add)** button in the bottom right corner or the add button in the top right corner of the screen.
        *   Enter the **Extracurricular Name** (e.g., Futsal, Scouts, Dance).
        *   Enter a brief **Description** of the activity.
        *   In the **Supervisor** section, staff can select a teacher or other person in charge of the activity.
        *   Tap the **Save** button to complete the creation.

        **Editing & Deleting**

        *   **Edit**: Tap on one of the extracurricular cards, then select the **Edit** option to change data.
        *   **Archive (Temporary Delete)**: Swipe or select the delete option to deactivate extracurriculars that are no longer active.


2. **Extracurricular Schedule**

        This menu functions to set the activity schedule so it appears in the student application.

        **Viewing the Schedule**

        On this page, you will see day tabs (Monday, Tuesday, etc.) at the bottom. Tap on a day to view the activity schedule for that day.

        **Adding a New Schedule**

        *   Tap the **+ (Add)** button.
        *   Select the **Extracurricular** you want to schedule.
        *   Specify the **Day**, **Start Time**, and **End Time**.
        *   Save the schedule.


3. **Member List (Registration Validation)**

        Students who register through their application will enter this list with a *"Pending"* status (Waiting for Approval). Staff are responsible for verifying these registrations.

        **How to Approve/Reject Applicants**

        *   Open the **Member List** menu.
        *   Use the **Filter** feature (funnel icon) to display only students with a *"Waiting for Approval"* status.
        *   Tap on the student's name card. A confirmation dialog will appear.
        *   Select **Approve** to accept the student, or **Reject** if they do not meet the requirements.

        > **Info:** Students who have not been approved will not be able to see the full schedule or perform attendance.

4. **Extracurricular Attendance**

        This feature is used during the activity to record student attendance.

        **How to Record Attendance**

        *   Open the **Extracurricular Attendance** menu.
        *   Select the **Date** of the activity (defaults to today).
        *   Select the **Extracurricular** currently in progress. A list of official student members will appear.
        *   Mark each student's attendance status:
            *   **Present** (green check icon)
            *   **Sick** (thermometer/medical icon)
            *   **Permission** (letter/document icon)
            *   **Absent** (red cross icon)
        *   Once finished, tap the **Save Attendance** button.

        > **Important:** Ensure attendance data is entered correctly as it will be saved in the student's attendance record.
