The Teacher Schedule feature allows you to monitor your personal teaching schedule and view your colleagues' schedules. With this feature, you can know when your teaching time is, as well as check other teachers' schedules for coordination or school administration purposes.

# Key Features

1. **My Schedule**

        Personal agenda menu to view your daily teaching schedule.

        *   **Day Navigation**: Horizontal tabs (Monday - Sunday) to quickly switch between days.
        *   **Default Today**: The app automatically displays today's schedule when first opened.
        *   **Active Day Highlight**: The selected day is marked with a special color for easy navigation.

2. **Complete Schedule Information**

        Each schedule card displays detailed information:

        *   **Subject**: Name of the subject being taught (example: Mathematics, Indonesian Language).
        *   **Class**: Class name and section where teaching takes place (example: XII RPL B, XII TKJ D).
        *   **Teaching Time**: Start and end time of the teaching session (example: 08:00 - 09:30).
        *   **Additional Notes**: Special messages or instructions if any (example: "Bring teaching aids", "Midterm exam").

3. **Teacher Schedule**

        Feature to view other teachers' teaching schedules.

        *   **Teacher Profile**: Displays photo and full name of the selected teacher.
        *   **Profile Photo Zoom**: Tap the photo to enlarge with pinch-to-zoom feature.
        *   **Hero Animation**: Smooth photo transition when opening schedule details.
        *   **Day Navigation**: Horizontal tabs to view teacher's schedule on specific days.

---

## Benefits

*   **Teaching Preparation**: Quickly know daily teaching schedule to prepare teaching materials.
*   **Activity Coordination**: Find colleagues' free time for meetings or discussions.
*   **Teacher Replacement**: Know the schedule of teachers to be replaced when unable to attend.
*   **Time Management**: Avoid being late or forgetting teaching schedule.
*   **School Administration**: Help staff organize school activities and coordinate between teachers.
