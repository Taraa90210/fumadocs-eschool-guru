The Class Schedule feature allows you to view all lesson schedules taking place in a specific class or study group (rombel). With this feature, you can find out what subjects are taught, who the teaching staff are, and when they take place.

# Key Features

1. **Class Selection**

        You can choose which class you want to monitor the schedule for.

        *   **Class Filter**: **Select Class** button at the top of the screen to open the list of available classes.
        *   **Class Search**: Search field to quickly find classes (example: XII RPL B, XII TKJ D).
        *   **Switch Class**: Can switch to another class anytime without returning to the main menu.

2. **Day Navigation**

        Navigation system to view schedules by day.

        *   **Day Tabs**: Row of days (Monday - Sunday) in horizontal tabs that can be clicked.
        *   **Active Day**: The selected day will be marked with a special highlight.
        *   **Default Today**: When first opened, the app automatically displays today's schedule.

3. **Complete Schedule Information**

        Each schedule card displays detailed information:

        *   **Subject**: Name of the subject being taught (example: Mathematics, Science, English).
        *   **Teaching Staff**: Full name of the teacher assigned to teach at that time.
        *   **Lesson Time**: Start and end time of the lesson session (example: 08:00 - 09:30).
        *   **Additional Notes**: Special information if any, such as notes for that lesson.

---

## Benefits

*   **Teacher Coordination**: Know who is teaching in a specific class for coordination purposes.
*   **Classroom Monitoring**: Check classroom availability for planning additional activities.
*   **Teacher Replacement**: Facilitate finding schedule information when a teacher is unable to attend.
*   **School Administration**: Help staff organize and monitor teaching and learning activities.
*   **Activity Planning**: Avoid schedule conflicts when planning school events.
