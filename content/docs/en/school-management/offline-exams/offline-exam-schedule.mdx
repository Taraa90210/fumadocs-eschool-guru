## Organize and Monitor All Face-to-Face Exam Activities Efficiently

The **Offline Exam Schedule** menu is designed to provide comprehensive visibility to teachers and students regarding the implementation of offline exams. With a clean and informative interface, users can monitor daily schedules, details of tested subjects, and exam status in real-time.

# Main Features

1. **Real-Time Exam Status**

        Each exam currently in progress will be marked with a **"In Progress"** label, making it easier for users to identify active activities on that day.

2. **Date and Day Details**

        The system displays detailed schedule information:

        * Day and date of implementation
        * Start and end dates of the exam
        * Total duration of the exam implementation

        All are arranged chronologically for easy browsing, especially during dense exam periods.

3. **Integrated Classes and Subjects**

        * Display **participating classes** (e.g., XI TKJ - ID)
        * Number and type of **subjects** being tested
        * Additional information such as start time, end time, and **minimum passing score**

4. **Scores & Evaluation**

        Each exam is also accompanied by:

        * **Maximum Total Score** — to assess student exam results
        * **Passing Score** — as the competency standard to be achieved

        These scores are displayed directly on the exam card for easy result monitoring.

5. **Detailed Navigation**

        The **"Details"** button allows users to see:

        * Details of the subjects being tested
        * Implementation time per subject
        * Specific duration and evaluation metrics used

6. **Structured Daily Schedule**

        In a single view, users can see:

        * Exams that are currently and will be in progress
        * Schedule division by day and by subject
        * Colors and icons that distinguish exam status and important information
