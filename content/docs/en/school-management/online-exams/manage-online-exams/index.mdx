## Manage and Conduct Digital Exams Practically and Structurally

The **Online Exam** menu is the control center for creating, managing, and conducting digital-based exams. This feature provides high flexibility for educators in compiling questions, setting implementation times, and monitoring student exam performance in real-time.

<img src="/images/page/37_kelolaujianon.jpg" alt="" className="w-full max-w-sm max-h-96 object-contain mx-auto mb-6" />

# Features:

1. **Exam Filters**

        Use dynamic filters to narrow down the list of exams based on:

        * **Class**
        * **Subject**

        This feature simplifies searching for relevant exams, especially when handling many classes or exam topics simultaneously.

2. **List of Created Exams**

        Each exam entry displays essential information concisely, including:

        * **Exam Title**
        * **Start and End times**
        * **Exam duration (in minutes)**
        * **Quick access** to **question settings** and **archive/edit** features

With a neat visual design, teachers can monitor exam status and make changes quickly in just a few taps.
