## Build Your Digital Exam in a Few Easy Steps

The **Create Online Exam** feature provides freedom in defining exam parameters in detail with an efficient and clear form layout.

<img src="/images/page/38_buatujianon.jpg" alt="" className="w-full max-w-sm max-h-96 object-contain mx-auto mb-6" />

# Steps to Create an Online Exam:

1. **Basic Information**

        Define:

        * **Class and subject**
        * **Exam title**
        * **Exam key (for participant entry access)**

        Exam keys can be generated automatically or customized manually to maintain confidentiality.

2. **Exam Details**

        Set:

        * **Start date and time**
        * **End date and time**
        * **Maximum exam duration (up to 999 minutes)**

        This provides full control over completion time and exam validity.

3. **Finalization**

        Once all parameters are set, simply press **"Create Exam"** to instantly publish the exam to students in the associated class. The exam will automatically be added to the list and can be edited at any time.
