## Monitor Teacher Assignments in Real-Time, Accurately & Organized

The **Teacher Assignment Monitoring** feature in eSchool Mobile makes it easier for school admins to track and ensure all teachers have completed their assignment submission obligations on schedule. Designed with an intuitive and elegant interface, this menu brings transparency and efficiency to academic management.

<img src="/images/page/54_monitoringtugasguru.jpg" alt="" className="w-full max-w-sm max-h-96 object-contain mx-auto mb-6" />

# **Core Features**

1. **Filter by Date & Academic Year**

        Select a specific time to monitor, so that the data displayed corresponds to the desired period. Ideal for daily, weekly, or even semesterly inspections.

2. **Automated Monitoring Summary**

        A quick view of the number of teachers monitored:

        * **Total Registered Teachers**: The overall number integrated into the system.
        * **Visual Indicator**: A green progress bar showing the overall achievement of assignment submissions.
        * **'Not Yet Submitted' Status**: Identification of teachers who have not yet turned in their assignments.

3. **Teacher List & Assignment Count**

        Presented in an interactive table:

        * Full teacher name
        * Total assignments submitted
        * Detail access button to see assignment status per teacher

        This view helps admins perform quick follow-ups with teachers who have not yet completed their teaching tasks optimally.

4. **Benefits of Teacher Assignment Monitoring**

        * Ensures academic responsibilities are met on time
        * Identifies assignment submission obstacles early on
        * Encourages teacher consistency and discipline
        * Supports the educator performance evaluation process

This menu is perfectly suited for curriculum departments, school principals, or academic admins to ensure the quality of teaching and learning activities proceeds according to plan.
