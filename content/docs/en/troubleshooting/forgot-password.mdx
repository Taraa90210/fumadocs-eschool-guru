## Reset Your eSchool Account Password Easily and Securely

The **Forgot Password** menu is designed to help users who cannot access their eSchool Mobile account because they have forgotten their password. The process is fast, secure, and can be done directly from the app.

<img src="/images/page/74_lupasandi.jpg" alt="" className="w-full max-w-sm max-h-96 object-contain mx-auto mb-6" />

# Steps to Reset Password

1. Enter the email address registered in the eSchool system in the field provided.
2. Click the **"Send Reset Link"** button.
3. The system will send a **verification link via your email**.
4. Open the email, then click the link to reset your password.

## Important Note!

1. Ensure you are using a **valid email address registered** with your eSchool account.
2. If you do not find the email, **check your Spam or Promotions folder**.
3. The reset link is only valid for a **limited time**, so perform the reset process as soon as possible.

### Still Having Issues?

If you have not received the email or are having difficulty with the reset process, please contact **your school admin** or visit the [**eSchool Help Page**](https://www.eschool.ac.id/#contact-us) for further support.
