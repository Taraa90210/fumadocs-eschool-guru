## Easy Access to Your Monthly Pay Slip

The **My Salary** menu makes it easy for every employee to monitor their salary payment history in a detailed and transparent manner. With a modern and intuitive interface, users can check their base salary, net salary, and monitor how attendance or holiday affects the salary amount received.
