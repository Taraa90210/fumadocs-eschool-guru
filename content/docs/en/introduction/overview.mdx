# Welcome to eSchool Mobile

Integrated School Management Platform for a Better Future of Education

## eSchool Overview

**eSchool Mobile** is an application-based school management solution designed to provide fast, easy, and integrated access for the entire school community — from teachers, staff, students to parents. This application allows users to run learning and school operational activities efficiently, directly from mobile devices.

With an intuitive interface and responsive navigation, eSchool Mobile brings a modern digital experience in supporting a connected, transparent, and productive education ecosystem — anytime, anywhere.

## **eSchool Mobile Key Features & Modules**

Discover a variety of complete and integrated features designed specifically to facilitate modern school management. All available in one intuitive and efficient mobile application.

1. **Student Management**

        Manage student data thoroughly:

        * Complete biodata
        * Attendance history
        * Academic & non-academic records

2. **School Announcements**

        Deliver important information instantly to all users or specific groups (teachers, students, parents).

        * Exam schedules
        * Payment reminders
        * School activities

3. **Contacts & Directory**

        Access the school contact directory in one touch:

        * Teachers & staff
        * Parents & guardians
        * Academic units

4. **User Account Management**

        Manage access rights and account information easily:

        * Based on role: Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent
        * Login control and user security

5. **Teacher Module**

        Digital tools to support the teacher's role:

        * Input daily grades, assignments, exams
        * Monitor student progress
        * Create and distribute assignments directly from the application

6. **School Digital Payments**

        Manage school financial transactions without hassle:

        * View active bills & payment history
        * Upload proof of payment & receive automatic confirmation

**One Application, Total Solution**\
With eSchool Mobile, all your school's administration, communication, and academic processes are in one grasp. Designed to support education digitalization from elementary to secondary school levels.
